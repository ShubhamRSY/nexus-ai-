## Commercial Licensing

Nexus is available under **AGPLv3** (see `project/LICENSE`).

If you want to use Nexus **without** AGPLv3 obligations (for example: a closed-source or proprietary hosted service), you can obtain a **commercial license** from the project maintainers.

### Contact

Open a GitHub issue with the title **“Commercial License Request”** and include:

- company name
- intended use (self-hosted vs SaaS, number of seats/tenants)
- required integrations / support needs

We’ll respond with licensing terms and options.
