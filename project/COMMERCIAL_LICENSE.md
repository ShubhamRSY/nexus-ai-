## Commercial Licensing

Nexus is available under **AGPLv3** (see `project/LICENSE`).

If you want to use Nexus **without** AGPLv3 obligations (for example: a closed-source or proprietary hosted SaaS), you can obtain a **commercial license**.

### Pricing (annual, USD)

| Tier | Best for | Price |
|------|----------|-------|
| **Startup** | 1 tenant, &lt;10 agents, self-hosted or single-region SaaS | **$12,000 / year** |
| **Growth** | Up to 25 tenants or 100 agents, HA deployment | **$36,000 / year** |
| **Enterprise** | Unlimited tenants, HIPAA BAA, dedicated support, custom SLA | **From $96,000 / year** |

All tiers include email support and security patches. Enterprise adds priority support, BAA, and procurement-friendly paperwork.

### What's included

- Permission to deploy Nexus without AGPL network-copyleft obligations on your proprietary service
- Annual license certificate for legal / procurement review
- Optional white-label rights on Enterprise tier

### How to buy

1. Open a GitHub issue titled **"Commercial License Request"** and include:
   - company name
   - intended use (self-hosted vs SaaS, number of seats/tenants)
   - required integrations / compliance needs (HIPAA, SOC2, etc.)
2. We respond within **2 business days** with a quote and MSA.

Public summary also lives at `/legal/licensing` on any running Nexus instance.
